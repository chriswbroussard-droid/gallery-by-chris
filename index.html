Artist Statement

The Process

The work within the ChaoticColorsCollection is an exploration of fluid dynamics and chemical interaction. By manipulating pigments through controlled liquid reactions, the artist captures the precise moment where physics meets aesthetic form. Each piece is a documentation of energy—ranging from the microscopic behavior of cells to the macroscopic explosions of distant nebulae.

The Philosophy

"Art should be an invitation to wonder."

By removing the traditional brushstroke, the artist allows the materials to "self-organize," resulting in organic patterns that feel both alien and deeply familiar. The goal is to provide the viewer with a focal point of vibrant energy that transforms the atmosphere of any space it inhabits.

About the Artist

Art has been my shadow since I could walk, but it took a detour through 35 years of hairdressing to find its true form. Based in Austin, TX, I'm an experimentalist at heart who swapped hair dye for high-flow acrylics.

I use my deep-rooted knowledge of color theory to orchestrate collisions between chemistry and creativity. The result? Macro-organic patterns that celebrate the beauty of the unpredictable. I don't paint static images; I capture movement suspended in time.

Working with professional-grade acrylics and innovative pouring techniques, Chris creates vibrant, one-of-a-kind artworks that capture movement and emotion in abstract form. Each series represents a unique exploration of theme, palette, and methodology.
