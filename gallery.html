Aqueous Vibrations Series — BIRTH

The story begins with the Aqueous Vibrations series, where the first signs of life emerge from a fluid, rhythmic state. These pieces capture the initial movement of energy through water, representing the birth of ideas and the flowing, melodic nature of the subconscious mind.

Chromatose Series — EMOTION

As the energy stabilizes, it enters the Chromatose phase. In this series, the world becomes saturated with intense, vivid color. The movement from the previous series slows down into a dreamlike state, where the focus shifts entirely to the emotional weight and psychological depth of the color palette itself.

Molecular Flux Series — STRUCTURE

Zooming in from the grand scale of elements, Molecular Flux examines the tiny, invisible structures that hold the universe together. This series explores the tension and vibration at a microscopic level, showing that even in stillness, there is a constant, frenetic dance of particles building the foundation of reality.

The Elemental Flux Series — NATURE

The narrative then moves into The Elemental Flux, where the abstract colors gain physical weight. Here, the four core elements—earth, air, fire, and water—begin to clash and merge. This series represents the struggle of creation as natural forces seek balance within the frame.

Radiant Expansions Series — RELEASE

The journey concludes with Radiant Expansions. Having explored fluid birth, deep color, elemental power, and molecular structure, the energy finally explodes outward. These works represent the final release of light and spirit, moving beyond the canvas to fill the space around the viewer with warmth and light.

About the Artist

Art has been my shadow since I could walk, but it took a detour through 35 years of hairdressing to find its true form. Based in Austin, TX, I'm an experimentalist at heart who swapped hair dye for high-flow acrylics.

I use my deep-rooted knowledge of color theory to orchestrate collisions between chemistry and creativity. The result? Macro-organic patterns that celebrate the beauty of the unpredictable. I don't paint static images; I capture movement suspended in time.

Working with professional-grade acrylics and innovative pouring techniques, Chris creates vibrant, one-of-a-kind artworks that capture movement and emotion in abstract form. Each series represents a unique exploration of theme, palette, and methodology.
